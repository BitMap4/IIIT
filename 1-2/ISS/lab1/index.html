One of the most notable features of HTML5 is the native support for audio and video content. HTML5 provides the <audio> and <video> elements, which allow developers to embed media files on their web pages without relying on external plugins or codecs. The media elements can be styled with CSS and controlled with JavaScript, offering more flexibility and interactivity. HTML5 also supports various media formats, such as MP4, WebM, Ogg, and WAV.

Web fonts play a crucial role in enhancing the visual appeal and readability of online content. These downloadable typefaces, like Google Fonts or Adobe Typekit, ensure consistent and engaging typography across various devices. Web designers leverage these fonts to create aesthetically pleasing and accessible websites, optimizing the user experience.

Arc Browser is a user-friendly web browser known for its sleek design and advanced features. With a focus on privacy, it incorporates robust security measures such as built-in ad-blocking and tracking protection. Its customizable interface allows users to personalize their browsing experience, from themes to toolbar arrangements. Arc Browser also boasts fast page loading speeds and efficient resource management. Additionally, it supports extensions, providing users with the flexibility to enhance functionality according to their preferences. Overall, Arc Browser aims to deliver a smooth, secure, and customizable browsing experience for users seeking both performance and privacy.
